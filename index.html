Ramadan was started

The month of Ramadan holds great spiritual importance for Muslims, who view it as an opportunity for purification and self-discipline. One of the most prominent aspects of Ramadan is fasting, where Muslims abstain from food, drink, smoking, and other physical needs from dawn until sunset. Fasting is not only a practice of self-restraint but also serves to cultivate empathy and compassion for the less fortunate.
